Who We Are

Your neighborhood pool hall on Pond Springs

Mayfield's opened in 2024 with one goal: give Northwest Austin a real pool hall worth hanging out in. No frills, no attitude — just great tables, cold beer, and the kind of crowd that makes a Tuesday night feel like a Friday.

Whether you're an APA league player, a casual shooter, or just need somewhere to unwind after work, pull up a stool. Happy hour runs noon to 7pm every single day, and we're here until 2am when you need us to be.
