APA Pool League

American Poolplayers Association  ·  World's Largest Amateur Pool League

Monday Wednesday

The APA uses The Equalizer® handicap system to level the playing field — beginners and veterans compete on the same team. Teams play 8-ball and 9-ball formats, and top finishers qualify for the APA National Championship in Las Vegas with nearly $2 million in annual prize money on the line.

  • 8-ball and 9-ball team formats
  • Equalizer® handicap system — any skill level welcome
  • APA members play pool at half price at Mayfield's every day, all day
  • Top teams qualify for the APA National Championship in Las Vegas

Central Texas Pool Leagues

CTPL  ·  Local Austin League  ·  Start Date TBD

Nights TBD

Central Texas Pool Leagues is a local Austin organization bringing structured, cash-prize competition to neighborhood bars. CTPL offers a wide range of formats — 8-ball, 10-ball, Lagger's Choice, and Scotch Doubles — in singles, doubles, and team configurations. Skill placement is handled through Fargo ratings, and top players travel to tournaments including Vegas Nationals.

  • 8-ball, 10-ball, and Lagger's Choice formats
  • Singles, doubles, and team play options
  • Cash leagues with Vegas Nationals opportunity
  • BCAPL licensed instructor available for skill development
  • Fargo ratings used for fair skill placement
